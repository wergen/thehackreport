Tasmanian GovHackers deliver smashing success!

First-time hackers "Lorem Ipsum" have stunned the critics with a thrilling last-minute submission for GovHack 2014. The Tasmanian underdogs got off to a shaky start on the first evening of competition with several hours lost trying to agree on a team name. But they staged an impressive recovery the following morning over an inspired session of coffee and poached eggs to create The Hack Report, an online "newspaper" of dynamically generated Tasmania-relevant content based on a user's nominated search date. The Hack Report sources data from a wide variety of datasets Trove via API. The Hack Report is already drawing rave reviews with at least one team member reporting "My mum likes it." It's yet to be seen whether fame and glory await the group of hopefuls but one thing is certain: it was a great experience and we can't wait to give it another bash next year!
